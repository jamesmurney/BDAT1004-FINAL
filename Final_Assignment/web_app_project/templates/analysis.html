Conclusion

As per the analysis results, WildLife Department should spend $100MM to protect amphibians accordingly as:​

* $33MM -> Least Concern species as they are majority in number and to plan for better facilities. ​

* $22MM -> Vulnerable species​

* $44MM -> Endangered, Near Threatened and Extinct in the Wild species as they are most exposed to the verge of extinction in the future.
